# Research Project: 
An exploration into the learning effectiveness and interest of junior high school students by integrating role-playing games into organic chemistry teaching

The character in our game is an alchemist , and he is in a crypt. There are a lot of substances in this cellar, and he must apply chemical synthesis to produce a compound that can break the lock on the door. The player's mission is to control the alchemist to carry out a simulation of chemical operations using the gathered raw materials according to chemical principles. The game offers step-by-step instructions in the form of character monologues or task prompts that incorporate relevant chemical knowledge points in order to assist the player in understanding the synthesis process. In this game, players can use WASD keys to move forward, backward, left, or right, and use the left mouse button to drop items or produce new substances.
